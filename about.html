About PriceDock

PriceDock is a price tracking and comparison platform designed to help shoppers make smarter buying decisions. We gather prices from multiple retailers — including Amazon, Walmart, and Best Buy — to show you where to buy at the lowest cost.

Our mission is to make price transparency effortless. PriceDock automatically tracks daily price snapshots and provides visual history charts, so you can confidently time your purchases.

The project is currently in its MVP stage. We’re integrating affiliate APIs and building out user dashboards for personalized alerts and product tracking.
